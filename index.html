ABOUT

Land of 10,000 Lakes

Born and raised in Minnesota, United States. Often refered as the "Land of 10,000 Lakes". Known for our cold winters, humid/mosquito infested summers, and beautiful fall colors. Though I have plans to leave, I always find myself wondering when I would come back.

Civil to Software

After working as a civil engineer for a few years I desired a career change, but felt it was impossible to do so. I began self-teaching basic programming. Through a fortunate opportunity with Shopify, I am now pursuing my goals to be a software developer/engineer.

Mountain People

Proud Hmong-American. Hmong people are often coined as "mountain people" from Southeast Asia. Following the Vietnam War, a large influx of Hmong refugees arrived in the United States. My parents were among the several thousands that arrived and now call this place home.
